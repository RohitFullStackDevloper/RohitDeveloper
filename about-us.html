About Us

We Make Your Every Moment Special

With over 10 years of experience, Akash Murodiya photography has become a trusted name in the world of photography, specializing in capturing the most cherished moments of life. Our expertise spans across wedding photography, including pre-wedding shoots, and extends to a wide range of events such as engagement ceremonies, proposal parties, and anniversary celebrations.

At Akash murodiya Photography, we also offer professional services for commercial and corporate photography, along with memorable captures for birthdays, baby shoots, and maternity sessions. Every photograph we take reflects our passion for creating timeless memories, with an artistic and personalized approach that speaks to your unique story.

Whether it's your big wedding day, a special milestone, or a corporate event, DG Photo Studio is here to make every moment last a lifetime.

Akash Murodiya – Founder & Photographer

I founded Akash Murodiya Photography in 2018 with the goal of capturing the beauty and joy of weddings. Over the past 24 years, I've had the privilege of working as a Director of Photography (DOP) on more than 50 short films and music videos, bringing that cinematic experience to wedding photography and videography. I now manage a talented team of 30 members at DG Studio, including photographers, videographers, and editors.

Together, we focus primarily on Indian weddings, constantly upgrading our skills and equipment to stay at the forefront of the industry. My passion is not only to capture the essence of the wedding day but to create moments that will bring smiles to the couple's faces, even decades later, through a mix of emotional storytelling and lighthearted, fun content.
